Advertising & Partnerships

Independent journalism, supported by partners who get it.

Quality reporting needs quality partners. Our advertisers help fund the newsroom — they do not influence what we cover. These are the four principles that govern that line.

01

Editorial independence

Advertisers have no say in our editorial coverage. We maintain a strict separation between news and third-party ads. Sponsorship of a section does not buy favourable treatment in that section.

02

Open to all viewpoints

Our advertising space is open to all points of view — cultural, political, social and otherwise. We reserve the right to review every ad and to reject any that violate these principles or our acceptability standards.

03

Transparency

All advertising is clearly presented as commercial content, visually distinct from editorial. The nature of any commercial relationship is disclosed so that readers always know when they are looking at a paid message.

04

What we won’t run

We prohibit advertising that promotes hate speech, discrimination or violence; knowingly misleads readers through fraud or inaccuracy; or violates trademark, copyright, libel or political-advertising law in the jurisdictions we publish to.
