Valentine Eluwasi founded The Mutapa Times in 2025 as a London-based intelligence newspaper for Zimbabweans in the diaspora and at home. He covers diaspora business, contemporary African art and the long arc of Zimbabwean culture. His recent reporting includes the Henkel Ventures investment in Ruka Hair and a five-part series on the Pavilion of Zimbabwe at the 61st Venice Biennale.
