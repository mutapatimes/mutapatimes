Tapiwa Hillary Phillipo writes on the non-profit sector, development and public policy for The Mutapa Times. His reporting follows how donors, civil society and government shape everyday life in Zimbabwe, from health and education to land, livelihoods and the organisations working at community level.

He has a particular interest in how aid and policy translate into results on the ground, and in holding both funders and institutions to account for what their money and their decisions actually deliver.
