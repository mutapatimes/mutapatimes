Tapiwa Hillary Phillipo is a graduate of The Mutapa Times Academy. He holds a Bachelor of Arts (Honours) degree in History and International Studies from Midlands State University, where he graduated in the Upper Second division.

Before turning to journalism, he worked in public relations, corporate communications, records management and data analysis, including roles at Zimbabwe's Central Vehicle Registry and at Genopath Laboratory. He writes from Harare, in English and Shona, with a focus on the organisations and communities working to improve everyday life across Zimbabwe.
