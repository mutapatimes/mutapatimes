He covers banking and finance across Southern Africa for The Mutapa Times, from monetary policy and currency markets to the banks, microfinanciers and capital-market reforms shaping how money moves through the region.

He is studying Economics and Finance with Banking at the University of Portsmouth, and writes with a particular interest in financial inclusion, market infrastructure and the institutions connecting Zimbabwe and its neighbours to global capital.
