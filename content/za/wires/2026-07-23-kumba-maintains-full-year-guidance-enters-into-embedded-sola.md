Iron-ore miner Kumba Iron Ore's Sishen Iron Ore Company (SIOC) has entered into an energy offtake agreement with Envusa Energy for the embedded on-site supply of electricity to the Sishen mine, in the Northern Cape, from the Sishen solar PV plant. The project entails the development of a solar PV facility located atop the already built G80 waste rock dump within the Sishen mine boundary. The first electrons from the project are expected to flow in the fourth quarter of 2027.
