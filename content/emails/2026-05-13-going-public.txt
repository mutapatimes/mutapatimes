Subject: Two changes, and we're going public.


Friends,

Quick one. Thank you.

You signed up to The Mutapa Times before we asked anyone outside
our circle to. You forwarded briefings on WhatsApp, told friends,
told family. That's how we got here:

    ·  3,000 monthly visitors to mutapatimes.com
    ·  183 email subscribers
    ·  $0 marketing. No public launch yet.

You did this. Thank you.


Two things change from this week:

1.  Two briefings a week, not three.
    Three was too much — content repeated, inboxes filled. Two
    will be leaner, sharper, only the stories that matter.

2.  More local voices.
    Foreign press alone isn't a balanced view. We're folding in
    Zimbabwean publishers alongside the international desks so
    what lands in your inbox is the full picture.

(We've also rebuilt typography and mobile reading from the
beta feedback — open the site on your phone if you haven't
recently.)


And one ask: we're going public this week.

with one person, a quote-tweet — that's what tells the algorithms
