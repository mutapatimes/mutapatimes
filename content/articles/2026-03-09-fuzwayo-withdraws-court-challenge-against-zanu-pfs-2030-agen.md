Bulawayo-based political activist Mbuso Fuzwayo and his pressure group, Ibhetshu Likazulu, have withdrawn their Constitutional Court challenge against the proposed Constitutional Amendment Number 3, which seeks to extend President Emmerson Mnangagwa’s term beyond 2028. The withdrawal takes effect immediately.
