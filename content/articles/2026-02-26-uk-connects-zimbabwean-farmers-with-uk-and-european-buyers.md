(Harare) – Eight buyers from the United Kingdom and European Union will visit 11 Zimbabwean farm and food businesses that have primed their horticulture products for exports through their work with the International Trade Centre (ITC). The trade mission is organised by the UK Trade Partnerships (UKTP) Programme, funded by the UK government and implemented by ITC.
