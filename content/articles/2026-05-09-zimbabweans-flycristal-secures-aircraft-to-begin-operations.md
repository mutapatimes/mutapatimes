Zimbabwean start-up airline FlyCristal Airways has reached a major milestone in its growth strategy after securing its first aircraft to commence initial operational services. The carrier has acquired a 19-seater Let L-410 aircraft from Malawi-based Zebu Air, which will serve as the backbone of its planned domestic route network.
