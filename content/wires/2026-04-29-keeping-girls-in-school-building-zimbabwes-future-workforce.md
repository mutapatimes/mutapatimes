1 in 10 girls in Sub-Saharan Africa miss school during menstruationlosing up to 20% of the school year. In Zimbabwe, the World Bank's EAGER Program is confronting stigma, engaging chiefs, pastors, families, and schools to keep girls learning. By integrating menstrual health into education and economic empowerment, EAGER builds skills, jobs, and a stronger future workforce.
