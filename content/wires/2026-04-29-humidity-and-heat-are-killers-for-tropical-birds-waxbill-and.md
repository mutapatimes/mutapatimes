Rising heat and humidity are pushing tropical birds toward lethal hyperthermia, as blue waxbill and trumpeter hornbill studies in southern Africa show. Experiments and models reveal that humid conditions sharply reduce birds’ ability to cool themselves, driving mass die-offs.
