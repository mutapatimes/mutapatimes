HARARE — Zimbabwe’s Government has signalled a decisive policy shift towards integrating the informal sector into the mainstream economy, rejecting longstanding perceptions that equate informality with illegality or economic dysfunction. Presidential spokesperson George Charamba said newly approved policy frameworks are designed to reposition the informal economy as a legitimate and productive component of national development.
