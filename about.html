About

Zimbabwe, from the outside in.

The Mutapa Times is an independent business and intelligence newspaper covering Zimbabwe from the outside in — foreign press, live financial data and original analysis, curated for the diaspora and the resident reader. We sell no political access. The briefing is free, twice a week.
