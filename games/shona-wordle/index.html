How to play

Guess the six-letter Shona word in six tries. Each guess must itself be a six-letter Shona word. After every guess the colour of each tile tells you how close you were.

Shona spelling

Standard Shona uses the same a–z letters as English with no accents. Digraphs (nh, mh, vh, sv, dz, bv) count as their separate letters here.

The wordlist

The starter pool is small and edited by the Mutapa Times newsroom. We add words steadily. If something looks wrong, write to news@mutapatimes.com.
