# Advertiser outreach — twelve emails

Each email points the recipient at a custom pitch one-pager at
`https://www.mutapatimes.com/pitch.html?brand={BrandName}`. The page reads
the `brand` query param and inserts the name throughout (mailto subject
included). The page is `noindex,nofollow`.

Tone is deliberately plain. No marketing jargon, no all-caps, no "we'd
love to chat" filler. The pitch link is the meat; the email is just a
clean handoff.

Pre-fill your own first name in the sign-off and substitute a personal
detail (the `[…]` brackets) before sending.

## How the link works

Every link above is a URL-parameter customisation of `pitch.html`. The
page reads `?brand=…` on load and substitutes the brand name into:

- the eyebrow ("A partnership note for [BRAND]")
- the headline
- the deck
- the "what we're offering" list
- the closing fineprint
- the page `<title>`
- the mailto subject of the CTA button

If you ever need a brand we haven't pre-built, just hit
`https://www.mutapatimes.com/pitch.html?brand=Whatever%20Inc` — spaces
URL-encoded as `%20`. No new file needed.

Page is `noindex,nofollow`. Visits aren't logged. If the brand name
renders wrong (unsupported characters get stripped), the page silently
falls back to the generic "your brand" copy.
