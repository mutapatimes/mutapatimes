Gateway Primary School

Gateway Primary School is a member of the Association of Trust Schools, Zimbabwe's independent school federation. Located in Harare, Harare.

What we know

Gateway Primary School is listed as a member of the Association of Trust Schools, the body that federates Zimbabwe's independent schools. ATS membership requires schools to meet a set of governance and operational standards.

This is a primary school. Pupils typically continue to a separate senior school for IGCSE and A-Level.

Location

The school sits at coordinates -17.7672°, 31.0292° in Harare, Harare.

Map: OpenStreetMap. Open full map.

Verifying fees and admissions

School fees in Zimbabwe change every term, and the currency mix (USD, ZiG, both) is school-specific. We do not publish fee, pass-rate or boarding capacity numbers in this directory because they go stale fast and we cannot keep them honest. Contact the school directly for the current quoted fee, the currency of payment, and any boarding, uniform and capital levies.
